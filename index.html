N&M United Construction and Tiles Corp.

N&M United Construction & Tiles is a New York based company and provides professional contracting services to all boroughs and beyond.

Our owner, Max, personally oversees all projects. Growing up in Southern Italy, he has always had a vast appreciation of skilled craftsmanship. Max came to the United States with a mission to share his culture through his work. Our focus on quality and ethics, is what separates us from the competition.

As licensed home improvement professionals, we will always provide you with the highest quality of craftsmanship. Our clients speak highly of our attention to detail, ethical work manners, and creative, outside-the-box solutions. Safety always comes first. No job is too small for us!
